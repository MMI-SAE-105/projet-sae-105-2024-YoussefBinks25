The Artificial Brain:

The Next Frontier of Intelligence

The artificial brain is a neural network model designed to simulate human cognitive functions. It lies at the heart of artificial intelligence research, reproducing processes such as learning, memory, and decision-making.

Intelligence Inspired by the Human Brain

The artificial brain is based on deep artificial intelligence, a branch of AI inspired by the biological functioning of the human brain. It paves the way for applications in robotics and medicine.
